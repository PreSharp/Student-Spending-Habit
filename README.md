#  Student Wallet: Income, Expenses & Financial Survival Dashboard

##  Project Overview

The Student Wallet Dashboard is a Power BI project designed to analyze students' income, expenses, spending behavior, and overall financial well-being. The dashboard provides valuable insights into how students manage their finances, the impact of financial aid, and the major factors influencing their spending habits.

The goal of this project is to transform student financial data into actionable insights that support better budgeting, financial planning, and decision-making.


##  Project Objectives

* Analyze student income and spending patterns.
* Evaluate financial aid coverage and its impact on student finances.
* Identify major spending categories.
* Compare spending behavior across academic levels and gender.
* Examine preferred payment methods among students.
* Assess students' financial sustainability and survival.


##  Data Cleaning & Preparation

The dataset was cleaned and transformed using Power Query in Power BI through the following processes:

* Removed duplicate records.
* Handled missing and null values.
* Corrected inconsistent data entries.
* Standardized data formats and categories.
* Validated income and expense figures.
* Created calculated columns for analysis.
* Structured data for accurate reporting and visualization.

## Key Insights

* Students spend significantly more than their average monthly income.
* Food, books, and technology are among the highest spending categories.
* Financial aid plays a crucial role in supporting student expenses.
* Digital payment methods are preferred over cash transactions.
* Spending habits vary across academic levels and student demographics.

## Benefits of the Dashboard

* Helps students understand and manage their finances effectively.
* Identifies spending trends and financial challenges.
* Supports better budgeting and financial planning.
* Measures the effectiveness of financial aid programs.
* Provides data-driven insights for educational institutions.
* Enables quick and informed decision-making through interactive visualizations.

## Business Value

This dashboard provides a comprehensive view of student financial behavior, helping educational institutions, researchers, and student support services understand spending trends, evaluate financial aid effectiveness, and develop strategies that promote financial stability and student success.
